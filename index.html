Toy story shop

      We are specialists in antique toys, old toys and rare toys for collectors, such as Classic cars, Vintage cars, Motorcycles, Trucks and buses, Aircrafts, Ships, Trains, memorabilia and even more antique toys (only by local contact) - many antique toys come from well-known factories and regions such as the well nown Erzgebirge and Seiffen, Madrid, Moirans-en-Montagne, Lyon, in Europe, Toledo, Boston ,Seattle, Miami etc, in USA, and many other places all around the world.

We sell old toys from circa 1800 to 1950. Our business has existed since 1990. We sell no reproductions and no broken items. In this page one can study different aspects of our sell report made between the years 2003-2005. If you have any questions about our products, please contact us via email - we will get back to you as soon as possible!
